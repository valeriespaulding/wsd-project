Cutie of the Month

Pictured, is Dutches; a blue nose, brindle Pitbull. This photo was taken as a pup, she is currently 3 years old and a mother of six. She gave birth to her litter on Feb. 15th, 2010. As a pup she was spunky, energetic and loved to beat up on the older dogs. She was quickly labeled the trouble maker of the house. As a mother, she is now still quite energetic, but much calmer. Her pups are even crazier than her and you will find them featured in the gallery. They arrived in the following order, the first at 4am:

  1. Scrappy: a boy, tan and white like his mother
  2. Junior: a boy, brown and white like his father
  3. Bella: a girl, silver and gold brindle
  4. Bella 2: a girl, light tan brindle
  5. Akiva: a girl, black and gold brindle
  6. Swisher: a boy, dark tan brindle

All of the pups are currently in good homes, unfortunately one (Junior) escaped from their yard and is still currently missing; we do not have any current photos of him. The father, Big L, is 4 years old; you will find puppy photos as well as current photos of him located in the gallery as well.
